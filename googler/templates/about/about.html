Business Messaging and Contact management

Business Messaging and Contact Management App (SA-SMS) is an application developed by Blue IT Marketing Solution with the purpose of improving communication within the organizations, as well as advertisement strategies. This Application is most useful tool for communication and advertisement.

Bulk SMS

This is most important feature that can improve communication within your organization. In this service you can be abled to send a text message to the large number of contact at a time. You can send the text message to your employee or your customers.

Advertising

This service allows you to send advertisement to the customers, by sending an order to the system administrator. The system administrator will check your advertisement and send it to the contact list that is on the database, the number of contact list will depend on the chosen product plan.

Survey

At this service you can send survey questions to the customers/clients or your employee and get the feedback, this help the organization on knowing what customer/clients need or what your employee need. You can also use this service to get information about the customers.

Contact management

Add your organizations Contacts in our Contact Management module and easily manage communication between your organization and your contacts, the module also saves communication messages between you and your contact automatically.
