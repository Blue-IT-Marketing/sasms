How SMS Surveys Works

SMS Surveys works by allowing you to create and set up your SMS Survey Campaign and allow our web application to take care of sending the Survey to your clients at the right time to maximise response rate, allowing you to collect :
  • Customer Satisfaction and Feedback Data
  • Product and Marketing Related information
  • Promotional and Competition related information from your clients and potential clients

Our Surveys can be run in one of two modes, through the contact list you supplied or allow us to use our own contact database.
