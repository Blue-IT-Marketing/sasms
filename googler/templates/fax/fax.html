Business Fax Solutions

Blue IT Marketing Business Fax Solutions allows you to create your own fax to email service, this allows you to own a Cloud Based Faxing Solution that is always on, and enable you to save fax paper and money.

How it works

in order to start using our fax to email services start by:
  • Login using any of the federated login options or through your own email and a unique password
  • Create your account , both on the account menu option and also on the fax account option
  • Purchase your Fax Credits, under the account tab
  • Upload your deposit slip as proof of payment
  • Go to settings and create your fax number, and your own email to fax email address
  • See instructions on how to Send a Fax

Sending & Receiving a Fax

Sending & Receiving a Fax is easy and can be done in four different ways, thereby allowing you to send and receive your fax from:

  • Any of your devices
  • From your emailing Applications
  • Business Applications
  • And Also from your Online Email Service example Gmail
