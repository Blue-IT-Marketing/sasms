How SMS Advertising Works

SMS Advertising allows you to Create an SMS Advert and allow our web application to send the advert to our database of potential clients, without any other input from you, the app also allows you to access your responses, and statistics allowing you to keep track of the impact your advertisement is having on your potential market.

here is what you can do to start using our SMS Advertising Platform

  • How to start using our SMS Advertising Platform
  • Login into the platform, through any of the federated login methods or your own email and unique password
  • Create your account , on our account tab and also our advertising account in this page
  • Purchase your SMS Advertising Credits through your account tab and then upload your deposit slip after you made our deposit through the Deposit Verification
  • When making a Deposit make sure that you make use of the reference number on your Proforma invoice
  • Create our advert and Start Schedule under My Advert tab, once your advert is created click on it and
  • Assign the total amount of credits you need the advert to spend from your advertising account
  • Check your statistics and responses to trak the progress of your advert
